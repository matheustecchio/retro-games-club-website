Our Club's Purpose

The Retro Games Club is a community of gamers, retro enthusiasts, and tech lovers who share our passion for the past and create a place where people can come together to enjoy the nostalgia of old games and enjoy new experiences.

Game Night

Join us every Thursday at 6:30 PM in our Fliperama Room to experience the magic of retro and old-school games!

Summer Tournament

Every June, we host a summer tournament. Everyone is welcome to watch live or on our YouTube channel. Club members can participate for free, while non-members can join for a small fee to support the event.

Xmas Week

Celebrate the winter term with Xmas-themed games and our exciting Secret Santa tradition!
